非洲豬瘟的威脅

高三學測英文 - 主題探討:非洲豬瘟的威脅

高三學測英文 - 主題探討 🐷

主題：非洲豬瘟的威脅 (The Threat of African Swine Fever)

1. 重點單字 (Key Vocabulary)

contagious (adj.)

傳染性的

e.g., ASF is a highly contagious disease among pigs.

fatal / deadly (adj.)

致命的

e.g., This virus is often fatal to pigs, with a very high mortality rate.

outbreak (n.)

(疾病、戰爭) 爆發

e.g., An outbreak of ASF would destroy our pork industry.

implement (v.)

實施；執行

e.g., The government must implement strict measures at the border.

border control (n.)

邊境管制

e.g., Strict border control is our first line of defense.

prohibit (v.)

禁止 (常用被動)

e.g., Travelers are prohibited from bringing foreign meat products into Taiwan.

awareness (n.)

意識；察覺

e.g., Raising public awareness is crucial for preventing the disease.

invade (v.)

入侵

e.g., We must do everything we can to prevent the virus from invading our country.

2. 核心句型 (Core Sentence Patterns)

句型 1: ... pose a significant threat to ... (...對...構成重大威脅)

說明：用來點出「某事物」對「某對象」具有危險性。

例句：African Swine Fever (ASF) poses a significant threat to Taiwan's pork industry and food security.

句型 2: To prevent A from V-ing, ... must be taken. (為了防止 A 做...，必須採取...)

說明：以 "To V" 開頭的目的句，並使用被動語態 (must be taken) 來強調措施的必要性。

例句：To prevent the virus from invading Taiwan, strict measures at airports must be taken immediately.

句型 3: ... play a crucial role in V-ing ... (...在...方面扮演關鍵角色)

說明：強調某個群體 (政府、個人) 在某個行動 (V-ing) 中的重要性。

例句：The public plays a crucial role in preventing an outbreak by following the regulations.

句型 4: It is ... responsibility to ... (做...是...的責任)

說明：使用虛主詞 "It" 來強調「遵守法規」這件事是所有人的責任。

例句：It is every citizen's responsibility to report illegal meat products and avoid online shopping for them.

3. 寫作攻略 (Writing Strategy)

第一步：分析題目與文體

題目 "The Threat of African Swine Fever" 是一個典型的「問題解決型」論說文。你需要先描述問題的嚴重性 (Why it is a threat)，再提出具體的解決方案 (How to prevent it)。

第二步：規劃段落架構 (Paragraph Structure)

這類文章最適合的結構就是「先談問題，再談方法」：

A. 第一段：闡述問題與威脅 (State the Problem & Threat)

  • 主旨句 (Thesis Statement): 開門見山，定義 ASF 及其嚴重性。(e.g., "African Swine Fever (ASF) is a highly contagious and fatal disease for pigs that poses a grave threat to Taiwan's economy and lifestyle.")
  • 舉例說明威脅 (Examples of Threat): 具體說明為何它很嚴重。(e.g., "Because there is no vaccine available, an outbreak would not only destroy the pork industry but also impact our daily diet, as pork is a staple food in Taiwan.")

B. 第二段：提供解決方案 (Provide Solutions)

  • 主題句 (Topic Sentence): 總結需要共同努力。(e.g., "To keep ASF at bay, the joint efforts of both the government and the public are required.")
  • 條列方法 (Listing Methods): 從不同層面提出解決方案，可以使用 "On the one hand," "On the other hand," 或 "Furthermore" 來區分：
    • - **政府層面 (Government's Role):** "On the one hand, the government must implement strict border control and increase penalties for smuggling meat products."
    • - **大眾/個人層面 (Public's Role):** "On the other hand, the public must be educated about the risks and take responsibility, such as not buying or bringing any illegal meat products from abroad."
  • 結論句 (Concluding Sentence): 在段末總結，強調合作的重要性。 (e.g., "Only by working together can we successfully protect our local pig industry from this devastating disease.")
