高三學測英文 - 主題探討 🌍

主題：過度旅遊的衝擊 (The Impact of Overtourism)

1. 重點單字 (Key Vocabulary)

overtourism (n.)

過度旅遊

e.g., Overtourism occurs when too many visitors crowd a destination.

sustainable (adj.)

永續的

e.g., We need to find sustainable ways to develop the tourism industry.

impact (n.)

衝擊；影響

e.g., The flood of tourists has had a negative impact on local life.

resident (n.)

居民

e.g., Local residents complain that the rent has become too high.

destination (n.)

目的地；景點

e.g., Venice is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

implement (v.)

實施；執行

e.g., The government decided to implement a new tourist tax.

restrict (v.)

限制

e.g., The city plans to restrict the number of visitors entering the old town.

preserve (v.)

保存；保護

e.g., It is our duty to preserve these historic sites for future generations.

2. 核心句型 (Core Sentence Patterns)

句型 1: ... has led to the problem of ..., which puts pressure on ... (導致...問題，這對...造成壓力)

說明：用來點出「因果關係」，並用 which 子句補充說明造成的影響。

例句：The recovery of tourism has led to the problem of overtourism, which puts great pressure on the local environment.

句型 2: Not only does ... , but it also ... (不僅...，而且...)

說明：倒裝句型，用來強調過度旅遊「雙重的」負面影響。

例句：Not only does overtourism damage historical sites, but it also disturbs the daily lives of residents.

句型 3: To strike a balance between ... and ..., ... must be taken. (為了在...和...間取得平衡，必須採取...)

說明：以 "To V" 開頭的目的句，並使用被動語態 (must be taken) 來強調措施的必要性。

例句：To strike a balance between economic growth and environmental protection, effective measures must be taken.

句型 4: It is crucial for tourists to ... and ... (對遊客來說，...和...是至關重要的)

說明：使用虛主詞 "It" 來強調「遊客」本身應盡的責任。

例句：It is crucial for tourists to respect the local culture and choose eco-friendly travel options.

3. 寫作攻略 (Writing Strategy)

第一步：分析題目與文體

題目 "The Problems of Overtourism and How to Solve Them" 是一個典型的「問題解決型」論說文。你需要先描述問題的嚴重性 (Why it is a problem)，再提出具體的解決方案 (How to solve it)。

第二步：規劃段落架構 (Paragraph Structure)

這類文章最適合的結構就是「先談問題，再談方法」：

A. 第一段：闡述問題與影響 (State the Problem & Impacts)

  • 主旨句 (Thesis Statement): 開門見山，定義 Overtourism 及其嚴重性。(e.g., "Overtourism, the phenomenon of destinations being overwhelmed by tourists, has become a pressing global issue, posing a serious threat to...")
  • 舉例說明影響 (Examples of Impacts): 具體說明造成的危害。(e.g., "This problem leads to several negative impacts, such as environmental pollution, rising living costs for residents, and the erosion of local culture.")

B. 第二段：提供解決方案 (Provide Solutions)

  • 主題句 (Topic Sentence): 總結需要共同努力。(e.g., "To tackle this challenge, the joint efforts of both governments and tourists are necessary.")
  • 條列方法 (Listing Methods): 從不同層面提出解決方案，可以使用 "On the one hand," "On the other hand," 或 "Furthermore" 來區分：
    • - **政府層面 (Government's Role):** "On the one hand, governments must implement regulations, such as limiting the number of daily visitors or imposing a tourist tax."
    • - **遊客層面 (Tourist's Role):** "On the other hand, tourists should be educated to travel more responsibly, like choosing off-peak seasons or respecting local communities."
  • 結論句 (Concluding Sentence): 在段末總結，強調永續旅遊的重要性。 (e.g., "Only by promoting sustainable tourism can we protect these precious destinations for the future.")
