Spring was in the air! The temperature was mild and warm, and my flower farm finally became vibrant again, filled with vibrant colors and floral fragrance. Flower petals fluttered in the breeze, carrying a sweet floral scent into the air.

I really wanted more people to visit my flower farm, which had always been my goal. Therefore, I opened my farm to the public and placed advertisements on social media platforms. However, the situation took a turn one day when a woman trespassed into the flower section.

I was thinking of reminding the careless woman not to trample on those flowers, but things started to get serious gradually. More and more people crossed the fences as they saw the woman merrily taking selfies with my adorable flowers. People kept trampling on my flowers, and the place swiftly turned into chaos. My flower farm was left in a mess.

It has been a long time since that terrible event happened, but I will never forget it. As the owner of the flower farm, I feel a deep resentment toward the people who were so merciless that they had ruined my flowers. I had even set up a sign with the words "No trespassing" on it, but the worst thing was that people just ignored it. What imbeciles those people were! As far as I am concerned, the government should raise fines and strengthen regulations to prevent people from deliberately trespassing on private property.
