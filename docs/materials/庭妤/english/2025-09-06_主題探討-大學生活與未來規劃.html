主題：大學生活與未來規劃 (University Life & Future Planning)

1. 重點單字 (Key Vocabulary)

major (n.)

主修

e.g., Choosing a university major is a significant decision.

career (n.)

職業生涯

e.g., This decision affects one's future career.

significant (adj.)

重大的；重要的

e.g., It was a significant moment in my life.

explore (v.)

探索

e.g., Students can explore their personal interests in clubs.

crucial (adj.)

至關重要的

e.g., Time management skills are crucial for balancing school and life.

balance (v.) / (n.)

平衡

e.g., It's important to find a balance between work and play.

acquire (v.)

獲得；習得

e.g., Preparing for the future involves acquiring professional knowledge.

participate in (phr.)

參加

e.g., Many students participate in club activities to expand their social networks.

2. 核心句型 (Core Sentence Patterns)

句型 1: V-ing ... is a significant decision that affects... (做...是影響...的重大決定)

說明：以動名詞片語開頭，強調某個「行為」的重要性及其長遠影響。

例句：Choosing a university major is a significant decision that affects one's future career.

句型 2: ...explore... and expand... by V-ing... (藉由...來探索...並拓展...)

說明：用來描述如何透過某個方法 (by V-ing) 來達成兩個並列的目標 (explore and expand)。

例句：Many students explore their personal interests and expand their social networks by participating in club activities.

句型 3: N. are crucial for V-ing... (...對於...是至關重要的)

說明：強調某項能力或資源 (N.) 對於達成某個目標 (for V-ing) 的必要性。

例句：Time management skills are crucial for balancing academics, club activities, and personal life.

句型 4: ...involves not only A but also B. (...不僅僅包含 A，也包含 B)

說明：經典的 "not only... but also..." 句型，用來強調事情包含的兩個重要面向。

例句：Preparing for the future involves not only acquiring professional knowledge but also developing problem-solving skills.

3. 寫作攻略 (Writing Strategy)

第一步：分析題目與文體

題目 "My Expectations for University Life" 是一篇典型的「個人經驗與期望」描述文。你需要具體描述對未來的想像，並提出實際的規劃。

第二步：規劃段落架構 (Paragraph Structure)

這類文章通常分成兩段，結構清晰，易於發揮：

A. 第一段：闡述想像與期待 (Describe Expectations and Imagination)

  • 主旨句 (Thesis Statement): 開頭直接表達對大學生活的期待與興奮。 (e.g., "As my high school life is coming to an end, I am full of expectations for the upcoming university life.")
  • 舉例說明 (Examples): 具體說明想學習的知識、想體驗的活動或想達成的目標。 (e.g., "First of all, I hope to acquire professional knowledge in my major. In addition, I would like to join some clubs to meet new friends and explore my interests.")

B. 第二段：說明最重要的事與具體計畫 (State the Most Important Part & Your Plan)

  • 主題句 (Topic Sentence): 點出你認為大學生活最重要的核心價值是什麼。 (e.g., "For me, the most important part of university life is learning how to be independent and prepare for the future.")
  • 條列方法 (Listing Methods): 提出具體的執行計畫來實現你的目標。例如：
    • - **時間管理 (Time management):** "I will plan my schedule carefully to balance my studies and social life."
    • - **培養技能 (Developing skills):** "Moreover, I plan to find an internship to gain practical experience."
    • - **拓展視野 (Broadening horizons):** "Finally, I hope to make friends from different backgrounds."
  • 結論句 (Concluding Sentence): 在段末總結，表達你希望透過這些努力達成最終的目標。 (e.g., "By making the most of my four years in university, I believe I can build a solid foundation for my future.")

好好規劃大學生活，為你的未來奠定基礎！🚀
