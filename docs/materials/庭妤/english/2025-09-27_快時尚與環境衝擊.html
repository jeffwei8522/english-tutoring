高三學測英文 - 主題探討:快時尚與環境衝擊

高三學測英文 - 主題探討 👕

主題：快時尚與環境衝擊 (Fast Fashion and Its Environmental Impact)

1. 重點單字 (Key Vocabulary)

sustainable (adj.)

永續的

e.g., We should support brands that use sustainable materials.

consumption (n.)

消耗；消費

e.g., The fast fashion industry encourages excessive consumption.

textile (n.)

紡織品

e.g., A huge amount of textile waste ends up in landfills every year.

disposable (adj.)

拋棄式的

e.g., Many people treat cheap clothes as disposable items.

impact (n.)

衝擊；影響

e.g., The environmental impact of producing clothes is enormous.

awareness (n.)

意識；察覺

e.g., Raising public awareness is the first step to solving the problem.

eco-friendly (adj.)

環保的

e.g., Consumers are encouraged to choose eco-friendly products.

reduce (v.)

減少

e.g., We can reduce waste by buying second-hand clothing.

2. 核心句型 (Core Sentence Patterns)

句型 1: ..., characterized by A and B, has a significant impact on ... (以A和B為特徵的...，對...有顯著影響)

說明：使用 "characterized by" 說明事物的特性，並用 "has a significant impact on" 點出其影響。

例句：Fast fashion, characterized by low prices and rapid production, has a significant impact on the environment.

句型 2: The production of ... consumes vast amounts of ..., leading to ... (...的生產消耗大量的...，導致...)

說明：用來說明製造過程的資源消耗及其直接後果 (leading to + N./V-ing)。

例句：The production of a single T-shirt consumes vast amounts of water, leading to severe water scarcity.

句型 3: To address this issue, consumers should ..., such as V-ing A and V-ing B. (為了解決此問題，消費者應...，例如...和...)

說明：提出解決方案，並用 "such as" 加上動名詞 (V-ing) 來列舉具體作法。

例句：To address this issue, consumers should change their habits, such as buying fewer items and repairing old clothes.

句型 4: Raising public awareness of ... is the key to promoting ... (提升大眾對...的意識是促進...的關鍵)

說明：以動名詞當主詞，強調某個「動作」是達成目標 (to promoting...) 的核心方法。

例句：Raising public awareness of the true cost of fashion is the key to promoting sustainable consumption.

3. 寫作攻略 (Writing Strategy)

第一步：分析題目與文體

題目 "The Environmental Impact of Fast Fashion" 是一個典型的「問題解決型」論說文。你需要先描述問題的嚴重性與成因，再提出具體的解決方案。

第二步：規劃段落架構 (Paragraph Structure)

這類文章最適合的結構就是「先談問題，再談方法」：

A. 第一段：闡述問題與原因 (State the Problem & Causes)

  • 主旨句 (Thesis Statement): 開門見山，點出快時尚對環境的嚴重危害。(e.g., "The fast fashion industry, driven by the demand for cheap and trendy clothing, has created a severe environmental crisis that can no longer be ignored.")
  • 舉例說明原因 (Examples of Causes): 具體分析造成此現象的原因。你可以用 "This problem stems from several factors, such as..." 來引導。 (e.g., "...such as massive water consumption, chemical pollution from dyes, and the enormous amount of textile waste.")

B. 第二段：提供解決方案 (Provide Solutions)

  • 主題句 (Topic Sentence): 用一句話總結這段的重點，即「需要消費者與產業共同努力」。 (e.g., "To mitigate this problem, it requires the combined efforts of both consumers and the fashion industry.")
  • 條列方法 (Listing Methods): 從不同層面提出解決方案，可以使用 "On the one hand," "On the other hand," 或 "Furthermore" 來區分：
    • - **消費者層面 (Consumer's Role):** "Consumers can make a difference by embracing sustainable practices, like buying second-hand clothes or supporting eco-friendly brands."
    • - **產業層面 (Industry's Role):** "Furthermore, the fashion industry should invest in recycling technologies and adopt more transparent supply chains."
  • 結論句 (Concluding Sentence): 在段末總結，並呼籲大家改變消費心態。 (e.g., "Only by shifting our mindset from disposable consumption to conscious choices can we build a more sustainable future for fashion.")
