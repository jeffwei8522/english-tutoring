交通安全與「行人地獄」議題

高三學測英文 - 主題探討:交通安全與「行人地獄」議題

高三學測英文 - 主題探討 🚦

主題：交通安全與「行人地獄」議題 (Traffic Safety & "Pedestrian Hell" Issue)

1. 重點單字 (Key Vocabulary)

pedestrian (n.)

行人

e.g., The new sidewalk is designed to ensure pedestrian safety.

unfriendly (adj.)

不友善的

e.g., The traffic environment is unfriendly to people on foot.

yield to (phr.)

禮讓

e.g., Drivers should always yield to pedestrians at crosswalks.

law enforcement (n.)

執法

e.g., The government needs to strengthen law enforcement to reduce accidents.

ensure (v.)

確保；保障

e.g., Better road design can help ensure the safety of all road users.

awareness (n.)

意識；察覺

e.g., Raising public awareness of traffic rules is crucial.

culture (n.)

文化

e.g., We need to build a traffic culture of mutual respect.

improve (v.)

改善

e.g., Everyone has a role to play to improve traffic safety.

2. 核心句型 (Core Sentence Patterns)

句型 1: ..., which is unfriendly to pedestrians, has been dubbed "..." (..., 對行人不友善，被稱為...)

說明：使用關係子句 (which) 補充說明主詞的特性，並用被動語態 (has been dubbed) 帶出稱號。

例句：Taiwan's traffic environment, which is unfriendly to pedestrians, has been dubbed "pedestrian hell."

句型 2: The fact that + S + V ... is one of the main causes of ... (...是...的主要原因之一)

說明：用 "The fact that" 引導一個名詞子句作為主詞，用來強調某個「事實」。

例句：The fact that many drivers do not yield to pedestrians is one of the main causes of accidents.

句型 3: ...should strengthen A and improve B to + Vr. (...應該加強 A 並改善 B 來...)

說明：提出兩個並行的解決方案 (strengthen A and improve B)，並用不定詞 (to Vr) 說明其目的。

例句：The government should strengthen law enforcement and improve road design to ensure pedestrian safety.

句型 4: V-ing ... is the key to V-ing... (做...是...的關鍵)

說明：以動名詞當主詞，強調某個「動作」是達成目標 (to V-ing) 的核心方法。

例句：Raising the safety awareness of all road users is the key to building a friendly traffic culture.

3. 寫作攻略 (Writing Strategy)

第一步：分析題目與文體

題目 "The Dangers of 'Pedestrian Hell' and How to Improve It" 是一個典型的「問題解決型」論說文。你需要先描述問題的嚴重性與成因，再提出具體的解決方案。

第二步：規劃段落架構 (Paragraph Structure)

這類文章最適合的結構就是「先談問題，再談方法」：

A. 第一段：闡述問題與原因 (State the Problem & Causes)

  • 主旨句 (Thesis Statement): 開門見山，點出台灣行人交通安全的嚴重性，並提及「行人地獄」這個稱號。(e.g., "The issue of pedestrian safety in Taiwan has become so serious that it is often called a 'pedestrian hell,' posing great dangers to everyone on foot.")
  • 舉例說明原因 (Examples of Causes): 具體分析造成此現象的原因。你可以用 "This problem stems from several factors, such as..." 來引導。 (e.g., "...such as poor driving habits, flawed road design, and a lack of strict law enforcement.")

B. 第二段：提供解決方案 (Provide Solutions)

  • 主題句 (Topic Sentence): 用一句話總結這段的重點，即「需要政府與民眾共同努力」。 (e.g., "To improve the situation, it requires the combined efforts of the government and the public.")
  • 條列方法 (Listing Methods): 從不同層面提出解決方案，可以使用 "On the one hand," "On the other hand," 或 "Furthermore" 來區分：
    • - **政府層面 (Government's Role):** "The government should enforce traffic laws more strictly and invest in improving infrastructure, like building more sidewalks."
    • - **個人層面 (Individual's Role):** "Furthermore, drivers must learn to yield to pedestrians, while pedestrians should also obey traffic signals to ensure their own safety."
  • 結論句 (Concluding Sentence): 在段末總結，並呼籲大家共同建立安全的交通環境。 (e.g., "Only by working together can we get rid of this notorious title and create a truly safe environment for all road users.")
